Forest Karts

Web Design

Forest Karts came to Big Boy Websites with an exciting new business project — outdoor go-karting in a picturesque Canterbury forest location. As a new business, they needed a website to show potential customers what they have to offer.

The website design was broken down into different sections — go-karting packages on offer, health and safety information, a reliable way to contact them and information about the team behind Forest Karts.

With the brief in hand, I created a fun and informative website that reflected the business they were in. Lots of fun little design details combined with forest and go-kart imagery complete with a mobile site to ensure accessibility on the go.
